Blue

Blue is the colour of the sky and sea. It is often associated with depht and stability. It symbolised trust, loyalty, wisdom, confidence, intelligence, faith and truth and heaven

Green

Green is the colour of nature. It symbolised growth, harmony, freshnes, and fertility. Green has strong emotional correspondence with safety.

Red
